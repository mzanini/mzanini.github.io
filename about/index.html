My name is Marco Zanini and I've been born in Trentino, Italy.

Based in Paris, I have worked for more than 3 years as a Software Engineer in Schlumberger, the biggest oil service company in the world.

I have obtained a bachelor and master degree in Information Engineering at the Polytechnic Institute of Milan and done my master's thesis at the Irish Software Research Centre.
